Paris Saint-Germain Football Club

PSG is a professional football club based in Paris, France. They compete in Ligue 1, the top division of French football.
As France's most successful club, they have won over 40 official honours, including ten league titles
and one major European trophy. Their home ground is the Parc des Princes.
